Our Story
Introducing Rooted.

A note from our founder

Rooted was created to offer our customers with plantable zero-waste candle and ceramic options that goes beyond providing beautiful home products. It explores the idea of repurposing, in an effort to make a truly environmentally conscious home.

Rooted currently offers naturally scented candles, candle refills, and ceramics. Our ceramic provides a visually pleasing home pieces that can be repurpose as a candle holder. Our candles comes in a plantable packaging box and multipurpose candle holder that can double as a plant pot. Rooted also offers a designated garden space in some Vancouver parks for those who would like to let their plant free and/or reuse the candle holder for the refills.
